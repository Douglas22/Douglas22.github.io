Ways to reduce your web sights running costs



The first notable way is using a different provider for your domain name and hosting many domain providers offer lower domain name charges even below cost as they can make it up on after sales such as hosting.

Many of these up sold services however can be provided by third party’s the main one being hosting.

To set up hosting point your hosting provider to your new domain name find the hosting IP address assuming its static as most are and update the A records with it.

Changes will take anywhere from 1 – 24 hours to take effect as the information has to be copied across multiple severs so don’t assume it hasn’t worked if you dont see a change right away. Insted take your time to get your head around it as you will have to wait a long time between changes.

Note that note all of these services can be easily supplied by a different provider such as having your private information masked from the general public (and web scrapes) witch you will likely wish to have for security so planing what you are likely to need is likely a good idea. It is possible to switch your domain name provider at a later date but means more hassle and often a added charge.



Another way is utilisation of external hosting for large files such as video content you see many sights using You tube for video content now they also have some really pretty clever infrastructure and technology for serving the files faster and more efficiently than your likely able to using things like Google’s VP9 technology (more efficient than flash)

there is the potential down side of excessive use of external links to content reducing search rankings but there’s also the upside of serving your pages faster and obviously reducing your hosting bill
